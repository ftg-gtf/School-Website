The Hungry Kids Restaurant started in 2010 with only 5 employees-3 chefs and 2 managers.We quickly grew to 10 chefs and we expanded to various cities including Hyderabad,Chennai,Mumbai,Thiruvananthapuram and Delhi.We have also been consistently placed in Restaurant magazine's annual list of the Top 50 restaurants in the world. In 2014, it was placed 41st,up from 43rd in the 2011 rankings. It was awarded a Michelin star in 2016.
